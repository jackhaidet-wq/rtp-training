CHEAT SHEET 11 - YOUR CLAUDE.MD BASELINE
Track 2 - Governance / Lesson 2.6
================================================================

WHAT CLAUDE.MD IS
  A firm-wide instruction file Claude reads at the START of
  every session. It's how every Resurgens employee gets the
  same starting Claude, with the same rules.

WHAT'S IN THE RESURGENS BASELINE
  - Firm context (who Resurgens is, the kind of work, tone)
  - The four-bright-line list (MNPI / deal terms / LP data /
    portco financials)
  - Output-style preferences (firm-format memos, brief tone,
    citations expected)
  - The "if you're about to do something irreversible, ask
    first" rule
  - Connector overview and approval expectations

WHO MAINTAINS IT
  - Will and Henry maintain the baseline
  - AnswerRocket helps version it during Phase 1
  - Resurgens IT deploys it to your machine

WHAT YOU DON'T DO
  - You DON'T write the firm baseline yourself.
  - You DON'T edit the baseline that ships to your machine.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
  - You CAN add a PERSONAL EXTENSION for your own preferences:
      * Your role and team
      * Your favorite output formats
      * Project-specific shortcuts you reuse
  - Personal extensions never override the firm baseline -
    they only add to it.

----------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO USE IT WELL
----------------------------------------------------------------
  - On Day 1 of training, confirm claude.md is in place.
  - When Claude does something off-tone, check the baseline
    is still loading.
  - When the firm changes rules (new connector, new bright
    line), Will or Henry updates the baseline; everyone gets
    it on next session.

----------------------------------------------------------------
WHEN TO TELL WILL OR HENRY
----------------------------------------------------------------
  - Claude doesn't seem to know about a firm-wide rule
  - Claude is producing outputs in the wrong format
  - You spot a gap in the baseline that the team needs
