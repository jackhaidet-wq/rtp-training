CHEAT SHEET 03 - PROMPT PATTERNS FOR PE WORK
Track 1 - AI 101 / Lesson 1.3
================================================================

THE FIVE PATTERNS - USE ALL FIVE ON IMPORTANT PROMPTS

1. ANCHOR THE ROLE
   "You are a PE analyst preparing a competitive landscape
    for an acquisition target. Cite SEC filings where possible."

2. CONSTRAIN THE OUTPUT
   "Output as a 1-page Word document. Sections:
      Market position / Top 3 competitors / Key risks /
      Open questions."

3. SHOW YOUR WORK
   "Before answering, list your assumptions and what you
    would need to verify."

4. ITERATE, DON'T RESTART
   "Tighten the Open Questions section to the three I
    actually need to ask the CEO."

5. END WITH A CHECK
   "Re-read your answer. Flag anything that depends on a
    number you didn't cite."

----------------------------------------------------------------
A FULL EXAMPLE - PUT THEM TOGETHER
----------------------------------------------------------------
  "You are a PE analyst preparing a competitive landscape
   for Cascade Holdings, a fictional industrial-services
   consolidator. Cite SEC filings or news where possible.

   Output as a 1-page brief. Sections:
     Market position
     Top 3 competitors with one line of differentiation each
     Key risks
     Open questions

   Before answering, list your assumptions and what you'd
   need to verify. End by flagging anything that depends on
   a number you didn't cite."

----------------------------------------------------------------
GOOD PROMPTS DO 4 THINGS
----------------------------------------------------------------
  [ ] State the role / persona
  [ ] State the output format
  [ ] State the inputs and constraints
  [ ] Ask Claude to flag uncertainty

----------------------------------------------------------------
COMMON MISTAKES
----------------------------------------------------------------
  - Vague tasks ("help me with this deck")
  - No format specified
  - Burying the question at the end of a wall of text
  - Asking for opinions without giving criteria
  - Restarting the chat instead of iterating
