WHAT M365 PLUGINS ARE
  Side-panel Claude access inside Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
  These are TOOLS, not connectors - configured under the
  moderate posture as part of training.

WORD - SIDE PANEL
  Good for:
    - Drafting from outline
    - Tightening, rewriting, tone adjustments
    - Creating a 1-pager from a long document
    - Adding a section that follows a firm template
  Watch out for:
    - Don't paste MNPI, deal terms, LP data, or portco
      financials into the side panel
    - Verify any number Claude inserts - especially in
      memos that go to IC or LPs

EXCEL - SIDE PANEL
  Good for:
    - Formula explanations and fixes
    - Building summary sheets from raw data
    - Pivot/aggregation patterns
    - Light data cleaning
  Watch out for:
    - Auditing computed numbers before sending
    - Confabulated cell references - check before relying

POWERPOINT - SIDE PANEL
  Good for:
    - Speaker notes from slide content
    - Re-flowing a slide into a tighter format
    - Drafting an exec-summary slide from a deck
  Watch out for:
    - Brand consistency (firm template still applies)
    - Quotes / claims sourced from somewhere - verify

WHAT M365 CLAUDE DOES NOT DO
  - No auto-send email
  - No shared mailbox access
  - No auto-approve on downloads or external sharing
  - No edits to files outside what you have open

----------------------------------------------------------------
ONE-LINE GUIDE
----------------------------------------------------------------
  Side-panel Claude is great as an editor / drafter / explainer
  inside the file you're already in. It is NOT an automation
  layer that reaches across mailboxes or drives.
